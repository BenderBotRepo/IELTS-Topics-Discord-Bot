**Weekends**
• How do you usually spend your weekends? What do you usually do on weekends?
• In your country, do men and women usually do the same kind of things on weekends (or in 
their free time)?
• When do you spend time with your family?
• What did you do last weekend?
• What do other people in your hometown (or in your country) usually do on weekends?
• What are you going to do next weekend?
• Is there anything new that you would like to do on weekends?
• Do you like working on weekends?
• Do you think employees should have to work on weekends?
• Do you think that weekends now are more important to you, than when you were a child?
