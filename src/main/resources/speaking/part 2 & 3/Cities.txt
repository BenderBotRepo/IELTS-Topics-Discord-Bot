Describe a city where you want to live in the future.

You should say: 
    what city it was 
    when you went there
    what you did there
and why you liked it.

***Discussion topics:***

**Differences and preference** 
What are the differences between young and old when choosing where to live?
What are some factors that attract people to settle down in some places?

**Population and moving**
Why have many cities become so densley-populated?
Is it possible that all of the population move to cities?
