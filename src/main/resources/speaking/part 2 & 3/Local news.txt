Describe a piece of local news that people were interested in.

You should say:
    what news it was
    who was involved
    where you saw or heard it
and why people were interested in it.

***Discussion topics:***

**National identity** 
Do you think it's important to have a national identity?
How can people develop their national identity?

**News** 
Why do local people like to read local news?
Why do local people like local news more than international news?
How often do people in your country buy newspapers?
