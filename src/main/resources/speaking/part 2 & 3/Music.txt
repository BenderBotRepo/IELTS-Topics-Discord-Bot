Describe your favourite singer.

You should say: 
    who this singer is
    when and where you listen to their songs
    what kind of music they perform
and why you like them.


***Discussion topics:***

**Music** 
What kinds of music do you like to listen to?
What kinds of music are most popular in your country?

**Parents and encouraging** 
Do parents in your country require their children to play musical instruments?
Why do some people like to listen to live music while others prefer CDs?
Do you think music concerts are suitable for old people to attend?
